Welcome to justForex! This is a platform to write and test foreign exchange trading algorithms in an easy, stress free way, and make money by getting investors to trade with your algorithms!


  • Algorithmic trading is using pre-programmed instructions to automatically conduct trades, based on factors including time, price and other market conditions.
  • The foreign exchange market (Forex), is buying and selling foreign currencies to try to make a profit. For example, if you are trading in Pounds, you could buy Euros when the Euro is weak and so has a low price, then sell them when the Euro is stronger and worth more.


Our site allows you to write algorithms in Python with which to trade forex, and to test the algorithms without using your own money.
